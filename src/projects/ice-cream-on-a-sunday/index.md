<h2 class="work__subhead">The photography</h2>

<h3>A changing style to evolve the brand</h3>

<p>While I started off with a very instagram-lifestyle look for the photos, I graduated to a more styled, minimalist view of the scoops to highlight the scoops themselves rather than the setting they were in. It was mostly fun to create the set ups.</p>
